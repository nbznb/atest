# AI News Summary Prompt

You are summarizing an AI or technology news article for technically curious readers.

## Instructions

- Write 100-220 words
- Start with the core news or claim
- Then explain the product, model, company, research direction, or industry move
- Include the most concrete details available: numbers, release scope, benchmark, funding, launch timing, or impact
- Do not overclaim beyond the source text
- Keep terminology precise but readable
- Include the original article link
